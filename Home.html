Explore Orchid Forest Cikole

Discover a magical blend of pine forests and orchid gardens in Lembang. Walk across the iconic suspension bridge, enjoy Instagram-worthy photo spots, and experience the enchanting night ambiance with glowing lights.

Marvel at Kawah Putih Ciwidey

Visit this stunning volcanic crater lake in Ciwidey, known for its surreal white-green waters and misty surroundings. A perfect spot for nature lovers and photographers seeking breathtaking views.

Experience Floating Market Lembang

Enjoy a unique culinary adventure at this floating market in Lembang. Savor local delicacies from boats, ride the Rainbow Slide, and capture fun moments at vibrant photo spots surrounded by nature.
